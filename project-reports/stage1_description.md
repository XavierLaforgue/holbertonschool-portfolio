# Portfolio project - Stage 1: Team formation, brainstorming, and MVP
## Objectives
- Build cohesive and effective teams with clear communication and collaboration dynamics.
- Brainstorm and explore multiple project ideas while fostering creativity.
- Select and define the MVP concept that aligns with the team’s interests, skills, and goals, or adapt to a pre-assigned MVP provided by an external source.
- Document the decision-making process, including the reasoning behind the selected idea.
## Tasks to complete Stage 1
- Team Formation
- Research and Brainstorming
- Decision and Refinement (ideation, risks, goals…)
- Documentation
## Deliverable
A "Stage 1 Report" should include:
1. **Team Formation Overview:** Team member roles and collaboration strategies.
2. **Ideas Explored:** A detailed description of brainstormed ideas, with reasons for rejection.
3. **Selected MVP Concept:**
	- Summary of the chosen MVP.
	- Reasons for selection (feasibility, innovation, alignment with goals).
	- Potential challenges and opportunities identified during brainstorming.
